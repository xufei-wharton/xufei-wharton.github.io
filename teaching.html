Instructor

Wharton Doctoral Math Camp
The Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania · Summers 2024 and 2025

For the past two summers I have been the sole instructor of math camp — the ten-session course every incoming Wharton PhD student takes before their first semester. I designed the syllabus from scratch, covering the real analysis, optimization, and probability that first-year coursework leans on, and have taught 40+ students arriving from economics, engineering, and everything in between, with uniformly positive evaluations. It is some of the most rewarding teaching there is: the stakes are real, the material clicks quickly, and you meet an entire cohort in its first weeks of grad school.
